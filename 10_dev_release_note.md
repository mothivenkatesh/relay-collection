# Relay — Developer Release Note (Beta Announcement)

**Audience:** Developer community (Discord, forums, dev newsletters)
**Stage:** Private beta announcement
**Tone:** Honest, founder-voice, community-first

---

Hey everyone 👋

We've been quietly working on something we're excited to finally open up for beta — meet RELAY.

**The honest origin:** merchants kept telling us that collecting payments was the easy part. The annoying part was everything after — manually flagging high-value transactions, cobbling together spreadsheet updates, nudging the team about failed payments, pulling end-of-week summaries. Repetitive work that somehow never got automated.

RELAY is our answer — and our first step toward an agentic payment operations layer.

---

## Automations you can build today

- **Transaction above ₹50,000?** Auto-log it to Google Sheets and ping your finance lead.
- **Payment failed?** Instantly notify the right person on Slack, WhatsApp, or email.
- **Want a daily/weekly payments summary?** Set it once, it runs itself.
- **Successful payment needs a follow-up meeting?** Hook it straight into Calendly or Google Calendar.

---

## Native nodes

Beyond external tools, RELAY ships with native nodes for:

- 🏦 **Cashfree Payments** — payment data flows directly into your pipelines, no custom glue code
- 🤖 **AI nodes** — native Gemini and OpenAI integrations to summarise, classify, draft, or decide inline
- 🧑‍💼 **Human-in-the-loop** — pause a workflow and route decisions to a human via Slack or Gmail before proceeding

---

## The agentic building experience

Here's the agentic part — how you build these:

Instead of clicking through a UI, describe what you want in plain English to Claude on your desktop, and it'll create and configure the workflow via the **RELAY MCP Server**. You can still use the Cashfree Dashboard for a no-code visual experience, but the MCP path is where things get interesting.

---

## Honest beta notes

- **Production activation is gated for now** — build and test freely, but going live requires a short access request. We'd rather do this slowly and get it right.
- **On pricing:** we haven't figured it out yet. Beta is free while we learn what's actually valuable to you.

## Why this matters to us

RELAY is a deliberate step beyond payments infrastructure — toward reducing operational overhead for merchants after the payment happens. The agentic building experience is just the start.

We're early, but the direction is clear.

And a small milestone worth mentioning — we already have our first merchant ready to onboard before we've even officially launched. That means a lot to us, and it's exactly the kind of signal that keeps us building.
