## Product Summary

Relay is Cashfree's AI workflow engine: pre-built agents for payment automation across D2C, edtech, travel, BFSI, and fintech merchants.

## Competitive Wedge

Razorpay built a marketplace of AI agents. Cashfree Relay is positioned as a workflow platform regulated merchants can actually use: vertical-deep, compliance-grade, and built inside the payments stack.
