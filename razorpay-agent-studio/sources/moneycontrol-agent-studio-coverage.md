# Moneycontrol Agent Studio Coverage

## Launch Coverage

**Source:** [Razorpay rolls out AI Agent Studio to automate payment ops, built using Anthropic's Claude](https://www.moneycontrol.com/news/business/startup/razorpay-rolls-out-ai-agent-studio-to-automate-payment-ops-built-using-anthropic-s-claude-13858737.html/amp)

**Published:** March 12, 2026

### Key Points

- Describes Agent Studio as an AI-led marketplace and builder platform for commerce/payment tasks.
- Notes workflows including abandoned cart recovery, failed subscription retry, dispute response, and cashflow forecasting.
- Quotes Harshil Mathur saying Razorpay wants businesses to run business logic on Razorpay, not just payments.
- Says agents can help small businesses avoid hiring 20-30 people to manage operational workflows.
- Mentions integrations with Shopify, Shiprocket, WhatsApp, Slack, Tally, and QuickBooks.
- Says businesses can create custom agents by describing tasks in plain English.
- Notes Razorpay plans to expand support for multiple AI models so merchants can choose models by need.

## AI-First R&D Coverage

**Source:** [Razorpay is not doing any R&D investment that is not AI-first, says CEO Harshil Mathur](https://www.moneycontrol.com/news/business/startup/razorpay-is-not-doing-any-r-d-investment-that-is-not-ai-first-says-ceo-harshil-mathur-13859189.html)

**Published:** March 13, 2026

### Key Points

- Harshil Mathur says Razorpay is prioritising AI across all new R&D investments.
- Frames AI as a fundamental shift in how business will be done in India.
- Mentions Agent Studio as automating reconciliation, growth campaigns, and payment recovery.
- Says AI token costs will rise with adoption, but frames that as a sign of customer usage.
- Reiterates future merchant choice across multiple AI model providers.
- Positions Razorpay as moving toward a full-stack operating system for businesses.
