# MediaNama Follow-Up: Razorpay Response

**Source:** [Razorpay chief product officer on AI Agent Studio pricing, compliance concerns](https://www.medianama.com/2026/03/223-razorpay-chief-product-officer-ai-agent-studio-pricing-compliance-concerns/)

**Published:** March 20, 2026

## Summary

MediaNama published Razorpay CPO Khilan Haria's answers to questions about pricing, price discrimination, information accuracy, and whether Agent Studio is meaningfully different from AI shopping channels.

## Key Responses

- Agent Studio is in early access with a free 30-day trial for merchants.
- Post-trial pricing depends on the specific agent and may be per-action, monthly fee, or outcome-based.
- Razorpay's argument is that many workflows currently have no free alternative: businesses either handle work manually, hire teams, or lose revenue.
- Agents do not independently determine prices or invent discounts.
- Merchant-configured coupon and discount rules define which discounts are available, which segments are eligible, and what maximum discount bands apply.
- Agents use structured, first-party data from merchant systems such as Shopify rather than external or unverified sources.
- Razorpay says it runs platform-level validations before every agent action, including compliance checks, PII handling, amount calculations, and out-of-scope behavior checks.
- The company frames Agent Studio as an operational layer for existing merchant workflows, not as a new shopping destination or consumer discovery channel.
