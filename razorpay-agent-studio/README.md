## Working Summary

Razorpay Agent Studio is positioned as an AI-agent marketplace and builder platform embedded inside Razorpay, focused on payments, revenue operations, and post-payment workflows. Public material frames it as a shift from dashboards and manual operating queues toward agents that observe payment/business signals, reason with merchant-approved context, and take bounded actions such as dispute response, abandoned cart recovery, subscription recovery, cashflow forecasting, RTO prevention, and settlement insights.

Razorpay's related Agentic Platform material positions Agent Studio as part of a broader AI-native merchant experience spanning onboarding, integrations, analytics, support, and operational decision-making.

Critical coverage raises open questions around agent-led price discrimination, dark patterns, hallucination risk, delegated consent, merchant economics, and governance for payment/commerce agents.

Follow-up reporting and Razorpay's own responses clarify the intended control model: merchant-defined permissions, first-party connected data, platform-level validation, audit trails, review-first modes, and pricing that can vary by agent as usage-based, subscription-based, or outcome-based.
