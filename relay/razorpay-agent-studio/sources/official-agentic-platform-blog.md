# Official Agentic Platform Blog

**Source:** [Reimagining Merchant Experience with the Razorpay Agentic Platform](https://razorpay.com/blog/razorpay-agentic-platform/)

**Published:** March 12, 2026

## Key Points

- Introduces Razorpay Agentic Platform as an AI-native merchant experience across onboarding, integration, analytics, and support.
- Frames the product around moving away from instruction-heavy dashboards toward systems that understand merchant intent, reason through complexity, and take action.
- Introduces RazorSense as a design system built for an agentic environment.
- Describes conversational, near-instant onboarding using PAN validation, CKYC records, and website-based business category detection.
- Describes Agentic Integration as a way to reduce developer effort by detecting the merchant's app or website stack and helping connect Razorpay faster.
- Repositions the dashboard as a control center for decisions rather than a place to manually download, manipulate, or verify data.

## Dashboard Examples Mentioned

- Intelligent Reconciliation: Upload a bank statement screenshot; the agent extracts UTR numbers and amounts, then checks them against Razorpay records.
- Active Revenue Recovery: Upload a failed-payment customer complaint screenshot; the agent identifies the transaction, explains the bank decline, and suggests fixes such as a WhatsApp payment link.
- Autonomous Guardrails: Merchants can set future rules for failed-transaction recovery flows.

## Relationship to Agent Studio

The blog describes Agent Studio as an expansion of the broader Agentic Platform. Agentic Platform is the intelligent foundation and merchant experience layer; Agent Studio is described as a curated hub for purpose-built agents handling specialized operational problems such as revenue recovery, dispute management, and collection strategies.

## Positioning Takeaway

Agentic Platform is the broader interface and experience layer, while Agent Studio is the specialized marketplace/builder layer for focused agents. Together, Razorpay positions them as a move from manual payment operations toward autonomous commerce workflows.
