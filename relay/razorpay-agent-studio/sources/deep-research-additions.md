## Newly Found Sources Worth Keeping

### Moneycontrol - Launch Coverage

**Source:** [Razorpay rolls out AI Agent Studio to automate payment ops, built using Anthropic's Claude](https://www.moneycontrol.com/news/business/startup/razorpay-rolls-out-ai-agent-studio-to-automate-payment-ops-built-using-anthropic-s-claude-13858737.html/amp)

**Why it matters:** Includes Harshil Mathur's framing that Razorpay wants businesses to run business logic on Razorpay, not just payments. It also says agents could help small businesses avoid hiring large teams for workflows like abandoned cart recovery, failed subscriptions, disputes, and cashflow forecasting.

### Moneycontrol - AI-First R&D Interview

**Source:** [Razorpay is not doing any R&D investment that is not AI-first, says CEO Harshil Mathur](https://www.moneycontrol.com/news/business/startup/razorpay-is-not-doing-any-r-d-investment-that-is-not-ai-first-says-ceo-harshil-mathur-13859189.html)

**Published:** March 13, 2026

**Why it matters:** Expands the company-level strategy around Agent Studio. Harshil says Razorpay is prioritising AI across R&D, AI costs scale with adoption, and Razorpay plans to work with multiple AI model providers so merchants can eventually choose models based on need.

### Inc42 - Strategy Feature

**Source:** [Razorpay's Biggest Bet: From Payments To Becoming The AI Brain For India's Small Businesses](https://inc42.com/features/razorpays-biggest-bet-from-payments-to-becoming-the-ai-brain-for-indias-small-businesses/)

**Published:** March 20, 2026

**Why it matters:** Strongest strategy piece found. It frames Agent Studio as Razorpay's move from payment provider to AI-powered business operating system. It includes commentary from Harshil Mathur and Rahul Kothari, mentions partners including Swiggy, Zomato, PVR Inox, BigBasket, LinkedIn, Anthropic, OpenAI, Nvidia, Emergent, and ElevenLabs, and describes the cost model as on-demand agents that activate on transaction/event triggers.

### Inc42 - Agentic AI Control Layer

**Source:** [The Control Layer: Why Agentic AI Frameworks Are The Next Big Thing](https://inc42.com/features/the-control-layer-why-agentic-ai-frameworks-are-the-next-big-thing/)

**Published:** April 2026

**Why it matters:** Places Agent Studio inside the broader agentic AI framework market. It says Razorpay operates at the orchestration and application layers rather than the foundational model layer, and quotes Khilan Haria saying Razorpay focuses on making intelligence actionable in real-world commerce contexts at scale.

### MediaNama - Razorpay Response To Critique

**Source:** [Razorpay chief product officer on AI Agent Studio pricing, compliance concerns](https://www.medianama.com/2026/03/223-razorpay-chief-product-officer-ai-agent-studio-pricing-compliance-concerns/)

**Published:** March 20, 2026

**Why it matters:** Direct response to MediaNama's earlier concerns. Khilan Haria says Agent Studio is an operational layer, not a shopping destination; pricing starts with a 30-day free trial and then varies by agent; agents do not independently determine prices or create unauthorised discounts; and agents rely on structured first-party merchant data plus platform-level validation.

### ElevenLabs - Razorpay Connector / Customer Story

**Source:** [Razorpay scales outbound merchant engagement with ElevenAgents](https://elevenlabs.io/blog/razorpay)

**Published:** April 7, 2026

**Why it matters:** Confirms ElevenAgents as part of Razorpay's broader AI stack and as a connector on Razorpay Agent Studio. The story says Razorpay completed 35,000+ outbound calls across 5+ phase-one use cases with close to 28% connection rate. Use cases include churn recovery, feature adoption, and incident management. It explicitly says Agent Studio can use ElevenAgents for subscription renewals, cart recovery, and customer re-engagement.

## Search Takeaways

- The biggest missing piece was not another launch article; it was the **post-launch risk-and-response arc**: MediaNama critique followed by Khilan Haria's answers.
- Inc42 adds the strongest business-strategy angle: Agent Studio as Razorpay's attempt to become an AI business operating system, especially for small businesses.
- ElevenLabs confirms a concrete connector/customer-story angle with metrics, useful for validating voice-agent readiness.
- Razorpay Sprint 2026 connects Agent Studio to a broader AI-native payment architecture rather than an isolated product.
