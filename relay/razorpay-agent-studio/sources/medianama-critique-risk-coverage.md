# MediaNama Critique and Risk Coverage

**Source:** [Razorpay Launches AI Agent Studio, But Questions Loom Over Dark Patterns, Price Discrimination](https://www.medianama.com/2026/03/223-razorpay-launches-ai-agent-studio-questions-loom-dark-patterns-price-discrimination/)

**Published:** March 18, 2026; updated March 20, 2026.

## Summary

MediaNama covered Razorpay Agent Studio with a critical policy and consumer-protection lens. The article summarizes Agent Studio as an AI-driven platform for merchants to automate payment-related operations such as failed transaction recovery, dispute response, and subscription management, then raises questions around pricing, accuracy, dark patterns, discriminatory pricing, and legal gaps.

## Product Details Captured

- Agent Studio acts as a marketplace for businesses to customize prebuilt agents or build agents from scratch.
- Razorpay claims agents can analyze failed subscription payments, initiate retries, trigger customer interactions, identify abandoned carts, and re-engage customers with personalized nudges and offers.
- Reported integrations include Shopify, Shiprocket, WhatsApp, Slack, Tally, and QuickBooks.

## Risks and Questions Raised

- **Merchant economics:** If Razorpay charges commissions on completed purchases through Agent Studio, merchants may compare it against free or lower-cost alternatives.
- **Accuracy and hallucination:** Dispute Responder must correctly gather transaction and delivery evidence. MediaNama raises the risk that an agent optimized for merchant win rates could produce incorrect or false outputs.
- **AI-driven dark patterns:** Personalized nudges and limited-time offers could become manipulative if they create false urgency or pressure consumers into purchases.
- **Price discrimination:** The FTX demo's escalating discounts for an abandoned cart raised questions about whether different customers could receive different prices in ways that become unfair or discriminatory.
- **Legal and consent gaps:** The article argues that Indian data protection and tokenisation rules do not fully address delegated consent, agent-controlled data use, or AI agents making financial decisions.

## Questions Sent to Razorpay

MediaNama listed questions to Razorpay around:

- Commission or pricing for purchases completed through Agent Studio.
- Whether selective discounts create price-discrimination concerns.
- How Agent Studio verifies pricing, availability, taxes, shipping charges, and other commercial details.
- Why merchants would integrate with Agent Studio if agentic checkout channels do not reliably create sales at scale.

## Relevance

This source is useful as the strongest public counterpoint to Razorpay's launch narrative. It surfaces the likely regulatory, ethical, and enterprise-buyer concerns around agentic commerce: accuracy, accountability, consumer manipulation, price fairness, and payment-agent governance.
