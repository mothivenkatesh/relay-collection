# Inc42 Strategy and Control-Layer Coverage

## Strategy Feature

**Source:** [Razorpay's Biggest Bet: From Payments To Becoming The AI Brain For India's Small Businesses](https://inc42.com/features/razorpays-biggest-bet-from-payments-to-becoming-the-ai-brain-for-indias-small-businesses/)

**Published:** March 20, 2026

### Key Points

- Frames Agent Studio as Razorpay repositioning from payments provider to AI-powered business operating system.
- Says Razorpay's operational insight is that payments are seamless, but the surrounding workflows are fragmented and manually heavy.
- Notes that nearly 80% of Razorpay customers are small businesses, digital-first brands, and startups.
- Describes the initial Agent Studio set as four production-ready agents:
  - Abandoned Cart Conversion
  - Dispute Responder
  - Subscription Recovery
  - Cashflow Forecaster
- Emphasizes no-code/no-configuration usage: small businesses can describe what they want in natural language.
- Frames the cost architecture as on-demand: agents activate when transaction/event triggers occur and stand down when idle.
- Says Razorpay plans to let merchants choose which AI model powers their agents, allowing cost/capability tradeoffs by business type.
- Places Agent Studio in the broader move from payments rails to commerce infrastructure.

## Control-Layer Feature

**Source:** [The Control Layer: Why Agentic AI Frameworks Are The Next Big Thing](https://inc42.com/features/the-control-layer-why-agentic-ai-frameworks-are-the-next-big-thing/)

**Published:** April 2026

### Key Points

- Places Razorpay inside a broader agentic AI framework market.
- Describes Agent Studio as operating at the orchestration and application layers, while using Claude as the base model.
- Says Agent Studio lets businesses define multi-step workflows in plain English, connect systems like Shopify, WhatsApp, Tally, QuickBooks, and Slack, and trigger actions based on real-time payment events.
- Quotes Khilan Haria saying Razorpay does not build foundational models and instead focuses on making intelligence actionable in real-world commerce contexts at scale.
- Notes early access includes a 30-day free trial and that pricing can vary by agent/use case, including subscription, per-action, or outcome-based models.

## Relevance

Inc42 adds the clearest strategic read: Agent Studio is not just a product launch, but a platform-control bet around orchestration, context, governance, and monetisation of agentic workflows.
