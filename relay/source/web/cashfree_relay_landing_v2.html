What you can automate

Workflows for the moments that decide revenue.

Start with simple alerts and reports. Add approvals and recovery flows as your team gets comfortable. Every workflow starts from a payment event your business already understands.

Failed payment recovery

Send hourly failure summaries, spot spikes, and trigger customer follow-ups before revenue slips away.

High-value payment alerts

Notify founders, finance, or account owners the moment a large payment succeeds, fails, or needs attention.

Live finance sheets

Write every payment, refund, and settlement update into Google Sheets so business teams stop exporting CSVs.

Paid-order confirmations

When a payment lands, send a calendar invite, notify the team, or move the order to the next stage.

Refund and payout approvals

Route high-value or risky actions to Gmail, Slack, or WhatsApp before money moves.

Reconciliation exceptions

Flag settlement mismatches, group exceptions, and send the right summary to finance every day.

How it works

Tell Relay the outcome. It builds the follow-up.

You do not need to know the technical setup. Describe the business rule, test it on Cashfree data, and launch when the team is ready.

Describe what should happen

Example: "When a payment above ₹2 lakh succeeds, alert finance on WhatsApp and add the row to Sheets."

Review the workflow visually

Relay turns the rule into a flow your team can see, edit, test, and share before anything goes live.

Run it with control

Safe tasks run automatically. Sensitive tasks wait for the right approval in Gmail, Slack, or WhatsApp.
