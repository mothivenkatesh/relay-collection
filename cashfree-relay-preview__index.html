FAQ

Got a question? We probably do too.

The honest, preview-stage answers. Ask anything else →

What is Cashfree Relay?
Cashfree Relay is the first Payments MCP with workflow + human approval built in. It turns your Cashfree payments stack into an AI ops teammate that runs payment workflows — RTO recovery, refund triage, reconciliation — inside the AI client your team already uses (Claude, ChatGPT, Cursor, Gemini). One install. No new dashboard.
Why is “vendor-agnostic” the headline promise?
Because the AI your team starts with is rarely the AI they finish with. Models get retired, repriced, and out-shipped every quarter. If your access controls and approval rules live with the AI provider, every swap is a rebuild. Relay flips it: the setup lives with you. Switch from Claude to GPT to Gemini to a custom in-house model — your Cashfree integration, your permissions, your approval gates, your audit log all come along. One setup, every workflow, any AI.
What is MCP — in plain English?
MCP (Model Context Protocol) is the open standard that lets any AI connect to Cashfree. Think of it like USB-C: one shape that works across every device. Install Relay once, and it works in Claude, ChatGPT, Cursor, Gemini, or any AI tool that supports MCP — without writing a custom integration for each one. When the next AI tool ships, your Cashfree workflows already work with it.
How is Relay's architecture different from other agent platforms?
Open protocol, not a closed runtime: Relay speaks Model Context Protocol — a standard, not a proprietary SDK. The same connection works in Claude, Cursor, ChatGPT, Gemini, and any client that supports MCP. Inside the perimeter: Tool calls run inside Cashfree's PCI-DSS Level 1 environment. The agent receives scoped answers, never raw payment data. You own the stack: Your AI bill stays with the model provider you chose. Pricing scales with your runs, not your revenue.
What's the difference between Cashfree MCP, Agent Skills, and the Visual builder?
Cashfree MCP is the open protocol layer — one install, exposes Cashfree primitives (orders, payments, refunds, payouts) as tools any MCP-compatible AI agent can call. Agent Skills are Cashfree-specific knowledge skills your AI loads to write correct Cashfree code. Visual builder is the no-code canvas inside the Cashfree dashboard for ops leads who want to see workflows, not write prompts. Same primitives. Three surfaces.
Which AI clients does Relay work with today?
Claude Desktop and Claude Code (native). ChatGPT (with MCP support). Cursor. Gemini. Any MCP-compatible AI client. Because it's an open protocol, any new MCP-aware client gets Relay support automatically.
Will the AI ever take an action without my approval?
No. Irreversible actions — refunds, payouts, customer messages — are gated by maker-checker. The agent drafts. Your team approves. You can also set confidence thresholds: if the agent's confidence drops below your threshold, the action lands in your review queue with reasoning attached. Supervised autonomy is the default.
Does the agent see my customers' raw payment data?
No. The agent sees only the answers it's allowed to. Tool calls are scoped, audited, and run inside Cashfree's PCI-DSS Level 1 perimeter. Card data, raw KYC, sensitive fields are never exposed to the AI model — only safe summaries and identifiers needed to take an action.
How long does it take to ship the first workflow?
Under 10 minutes for any starter template. Pick a template, point it at your Cashfree account, run. For developers extending templates or building custom agents, 30 minutes to 2 hours depending on complexity.
What does Relay cost?
Free during public preview. At launch, predictable per-run pricing — published rates that scale with the number of workflow runs, decoupled from the revenue those workflows recover. The economics stay tied to usage, not outcomes.
What templates are available right now?
In public preview today: Calendar invite on payment, Threshold alerts, Sheets sync, Hourly failed-payment digest. Coming at launch: RTO recovery, refund triage, fake-order detection, daily reconciliation, payout orchestration, abandoned-cart recovery.
Can I extend templates or build custom workflows?
Yes. Every template is open. Developers can fork, extend, or build new workflows from scratch using the Cashfree MCP server, Agent Skills, and SDKs. Partners can package custom templates and resell them.
Is my workflow data exportable if I leave Relay?
Yes. Audit logs, workflow definitions, and run history are all exportable in standard formats. Because Relay runs on open MCP, your prompts and workflows are portable to any other MCP-compatible runtime.
